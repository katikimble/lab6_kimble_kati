Lost Time

So these will be the years that I lose,
A double-edged sword to swallow, sheathed & formidable,
Saturated silk grazing the blade of what we have become,
Warped metal & loss hammered over the years, forging a ceremonious familiarity,
I remember thinking we would always know each other,
Feeding each other's minds and ambitions,
Growing a seed of intricate candor, bonded by camaraderie and endured life,
Touching our faces in escalated splendor,
What an allusive mystery that sisterhood projects.

But time courses through the blood of expectation,
Carefully untying knots of promises we made over the years,
Our beautiful words weigh down the divergent pages we've fallen on,
Woven & bound, preserved as armored history.

Last Day

And I never thought there would be a last day between us,
Tucked away under spirited conquest and romantic impulse,
An electric current of inevitable promise, skin deep and red hot,
A seething reminder of time colliding with finite stories.

Sister

I take sips of you in, dear sister.
Plucked honeysuckles and marigold locks,
Golden innocence, equipped with a morality compass,
Guiding me due north, always in the right direction.

Your enveloping soul balances mine.
A respite and symphony of revival,
A magnanimous wraith, glowing halo, with depth and purpose,
Intertwined and parallel with my own spirit, touching,
Like electric impulse and sparkling fate.

You are everything I am,
And you are everything I am not.

Nostalgia

My mind unwound all over you, pouring out rage
And selfish perspective, illuminated sentiment
and fashioned nostalgia,
Like a massive storm, unhinged and self-destructive,
I roared, forcing my way through and out of your life.

Self Destruction

I am incessant insults,
Years of insecurity & rusted self-esteem,
Furrowed into a lump, hugging the back of my throat;
Sitting quietly at my vanity,
Covering up emotional bruises with expensive high-end make-up.

I am chips of blissful self-destruction,
chiseling out my body into a shell;
Eating enough calories for an entire day,
in a single sitting, drinking to black out,
maxing out credit cards, all while a fogged dimness grabs on,
digging hooks into my doughy sides.
I am recklessly unbalanced & weighted;
Sinking into the rising sea,
inevitably lost.
